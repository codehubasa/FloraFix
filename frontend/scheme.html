🌱 Agricultural Schemes

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana

Crop insurance providing financial support to farmers in case of crop loss due to natural calamities.

Ministry: Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

Eligibility: All farmers in notified areas.

Benefits: Insurance coverage & financial aid.

Visit Official Website

Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana

Improved irrigation access for farmers with motto "More crop per drop".

Ministry: Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

Eligibility: All farmers, focus on small & marginal.

Benefits: Better irrigation, water-use efficiency.

Visit Official Website

Soil Health Card Scheme

Provides soil nutrient information & fertilizer recommendations to farmers.

Ministry: Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

Eligibility: All farmers.

Benefits: Soil testing & fertilizer advice.

Visit Official Website

Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana

Promotes organic farming practices & soil health improvement.

Ministry: Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

Eligibility: Farmers adopting organic farming.

Benefits: Training & financial aid for organic farming.

Visit Official Website

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)

Direct income support scheme providing ₹6,000 per year to small and marginal farmers.

Ministry: Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

Eligibility: Small & marginal farmers with up to 2 hectares of land.

Benefits: Direct cash transfer in installments.

Visit Official Website

Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

Provides timely credit to farmers at low interest rates for agricultural needs.

Ministry: Finance & Agriculture

Eligibility: All farmers including fisheries & animal husbandry.

Benefits: Loan facility with interest subvention.

Visit Official Website
